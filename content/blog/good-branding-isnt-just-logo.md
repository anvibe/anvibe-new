If you think branding is just about getting a nice logo, you're missing the bigger picture. A logo is important, but it's just one piece of a much larger puzzle.

## What Branding Really Is

True branding encompasses:

- **Visual Identity**: Your logo, colors, typography, and imagery
- **Voice & Tone**: How you communicate with your audience
- **Experience**: Every touchpoint where people interact with your brand
- **Values**: What you stand for and how you show it
- **Consistency**: Maintaining coherence across all channels
